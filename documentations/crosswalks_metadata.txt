**Title:**  
Population-Weighted Crosswalks between 2020 Commuting Zones and Historical Geographies (1990–2020)

**Description:**  
This dataset provides population-weighted crosswalks from 2020 Commuting Zones (CZ20) to multiple historical and contemporary geographies: 1990 commuting zones, 2000 and 2010 ERS commuting zones, and 2010 & 2020 PUMAs. Crosswalks were constructed using a 100m population raster derived from the 2020 Census. Each record reports population overlap, shares, and totals that allow consistent reallocation of data between geographies.

**Files:**  
- `cz20_to_cz90_popgrid100m_area.csv` – CZ20 → CZ90  
- `cz20_to_puma20_popgrid100m_area.csv` – CZ20 → PUMA20  
- `cz20_to_puma2010_popgrid100m_area.csv` – CZ20 → PUMA2010  
- `cz20_to_cz2010_popgrid100m_area.csv` – CZ20 → CZ2010  
- `cz20_to_cz2000_popgrid100m_area.csv` – CZ20 → CZ2000  
- `cz20_grid_totals.csv` – CZ20 population totals

**Variables:**  
- `CZ20`, `CZ20_name`: 2020 CZ identifier and name  
- `<Target>`, `<Target>_name`: Target geography ID and name  
- `pop_overlap`: Population in overlapping grid cells  
- `share_of_CZ20`: Share of CZ20 population in target geography  
- `CZ20_total_pop`: CZ20 total population  
- `state`: State identifier  
- Metadata: cells, grid resolution, CRS, method

**Geographic coverage:**  
United States

**Temporal coverage:**  
1990, 2000, 2010, 2020

**Sources:**

**How to use:**  
To reallocate CZ20-based statistics into target geographies, weight values by `share_of_CZ20` and aggregate by the target geography.
